About

Did you know 1 in 7 people in Kentuckians struggles with hunger? COTTON TAIL food bank. leads our community to feed the hungry and conquer the cycle of need. We fulfill this mission through innovative programs, efficient operations and by partnering with local food pantries, shelters and kitchens to get food to people in need.

Did you know just $1 provides $3 meals to a family or individual in need? Consider supporting our neighbors facing food insecurirty this Giving Tuesday. Every dollar goes towards a hunger-free Kentuckians.
